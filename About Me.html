About Me

Digital Media (MA) Student @ UCF • Indie Game Designer • Developer

Hi! I'm Bihao (Brian) Wang.

I am a Digital Media graduate student at the University of Central Florida, focusing on interactive media, game design, and experimental play. My work explores emotional storytelling, minimalistic interaction, and playful design using tools like p5.js, Unity, Unreal Engine, and A-Frame.

Before UCF, I completed my undergraduate studies in Digital Media Arts and worked on several independent game prototypes and interactive installations. I enjoy turning simple ideas into playable experiences, especially ones that mix narrative, atmosphere, and light interactivity.

Interests

  • Indie game development
  • Interactive storytelling
  • Procedural visual art (p5.js, shader experiments)
  • 3D environments & VR prototyping

Skills

  • p5.js / A-Frame / WebXR
  • Unity & Unreal Engine (Blueprints)
  • HTML / CSS / JavaScript
  • Digital illustration & concept art
